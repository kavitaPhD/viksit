Chhattisgarh Anjor@2047 Pragati Portal

Chhattisgarh Anjor @2047 is the state’s ambitious roadmap for holistic and inclusive growth, aligned with the national vision of Viksit Bharat. Under this transformative agenda, the Government of Chhattisgarh aims to build a ₹ 75 lakh crore economy by 2047, the state targets a 15-fold increase in total GDP, an 11-fold growth in agricultural GDP, an 11.5-fold rise in industrial GDP, a 21-fold expansion in the services sector, and a 10-fold increase in per capita income—ushering in a new era of prosperity and sustainable development.
