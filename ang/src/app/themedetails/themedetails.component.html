Chhattisgarh Anjor Vision @ 2047 - Theme-wise Department Mapping

Theme Description Mapped Department
Powerhouse of Agricultural Produce & Processed Superfoods Chhattisgarh identifies agriculture as the backbone of its economy, supported by diverse agro-climatic zones and a favorable monsoon. The state is shifting toward a high-growth model by prioritizing livestock, fisheries, and the production of processed superfoods. By modernizing farming methods and expanding global exports, Chhattisgarh aims to maximize farmer incomes and lead the nation in agricultural innovation. Agriculture Development and Farmer Welfare and Bio-Technology Department
Hub for MFP & Herbals Chhattisgarh is evolving into a global hub for Minor Forest Produce (MFP) and herbal products by shifting from raw collection to advanced processing. By 2047, the state will lead the world in sustainable forest management, global branding, and AYUSH-led wellness, ensuring long-term economic prosperity for its forest-dependent communities. Chhattisgarh Forest and Climate Change Department
Titan in Industry Chhattisgarh stands as a titan in India’s industrial landscape, powering the nation’s growth through its leadership in steel, coal, and mineral resources. By 2047, the state is transitioning toward a future-ready model that prioritizes high-value manufacturing and green technologies, establishing a global benchmark for innovation. Commerce and Industry
Tourism – Celebrating Chhattisgarh’s Natural & Cultural Tapestry Chhattisgarh is a treasure trove of natural beauty and cultural heritage, enriched by its vibrant tribal legacy and festivals. By 2047, the state will lead in sustainable, community-driven tourism that celebrates its unique tapestry while generating prosperity. Tourism Department
Leader in Inland Logistics Leveraging its strategic central location and multimodal connectivity, Chhattisgarh is transforming into a national epicenter for inland logistics. By 2047, the state will lead with technology-driven, sustainable supply chains and world-class distribution infrastructure. Commerce and Industry
Expansion into AI & IT Services With power surplus and competitive infrastructure, Chhattisgarh is emerging as a global hub for AI, blockchain, and cloud services. Supported by a strong startup ecosystem, the state aims to lead India’s digital transformation by 2047. Department Of Information Technology
Land of Innovation, Skilled Human Capital & Quality Education Harnessing its young demographic dividend, Chhattisgarh is building a globally competitive workforce through accessible education and skilling. Strong policy intent and investment are creating a foundation for innovation and long-term socio-economic growth. School Education Department
A Healthy & Prospering Society Chhattisgarh is developing a holistic, digitally enabled healthcare ecosystem. By strengthening primary care and affordability, the vision for 2047 ensures accessible, quality healthcare for every citizen. Department of Health & Family Welfare & Medical Education
Leader in Sustainable and Renewables-Led Development Balancing growth with environmental stewardship, Chhattisgarh is leading India’s green transition. By leveraging forest cover and renewable energy, the state aims to build a circular, low-carbon economy by 2047. Energy Department
Art and Culture Capital of India Rooted in rich tribal traditions and cultural heritage, Chhattisgarh celebrates unique crafts, music, festivals, and cuisine. The state is positioning itself as India’s cultural capital by preserving and promoting its artistic legacy. Art and Culture Department
Infrastructure for Thriving Communities with Enriched Lives By prioritizing quality housing, energy, water, and transport, Chhattisgarh is building resilient cities and villages. Urban modernization and social infrastructure aim to enhance quality of life for all residents. Gramin Vikas and Panchayati Raj Department
Governance for Lasting Value Creation in Citizens Embracing “Minimum Government, Maximum Governance,” Chhattisgarh is redefining administration through transparency and digital systems. Citizen-centric, accountable governance is central to long-term trust and prosperity. Good Governance Portal
Premier Investment Destination Chhattisgarh is creating a conducive investment climate through industrial parks, policy reforms, and seamless facilitation. The state aims to attract sustained private investment to drive economic and social growth. Commerce and Industry
